## Key Terminology

**Contract:** Legal agreement uploaded to LegalGraph (MSA, NDA, lease, etc.)
**Clause:** Individual provision within a contract (e.g., indemnification clause)
**Playbook:** Set of legal guidelines, approved language, risk thresholds
**Risk Score:** AI-calculated risk rating (Low/Medium/High/Critical)
**Extraction:** Process of identifying and pulling out specific clauses
**Deviation:** When contract language differs from playbook standards
**Training:** Process of teaching AI models using labeled contract data
**Confidence Score:** AI's certainty level on extraction/classification (0-100%)
